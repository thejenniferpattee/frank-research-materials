Thank you for agreeing to participate in this research study about how college students feel about using digital tools to help evaluate patterns in their attention, focus, and mood.

You will be asked to fill out a short questionnaire about your willingness, comfort level, and concerns related to using digital or AI-assisted tools for evaluating attention, focus, and mood patterns. This study does not diagnose any condition and does not test a medical device. The purpose of this research is to better understand how students perceive this type of technology. What we learn will help refine the wording, design, and ethical approach of an AI-assisted screening tool for future testing.
